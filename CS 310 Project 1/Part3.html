Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. Its is the third of three portraits of the Riviére family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100x70cm
Home: Musee du Louvre, Paris
